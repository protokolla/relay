History

I used to (only 8 days) host this relay at DigitalOcean. It was not the best idea since there is 500 GB monhtly bandwidth limit that I ran into pretty fast. The experiment cost me 5€. I regret doing that. After hitting that limit I moved the server to home. The relay has 100/100 Mbps connection, 2 GB RAM & 2 vCPU (Ryzen 7 3700x). This relay had different info page.
